"Music for a Cause"

SMH is an interesting fusion of alternative rock where covers range from the upbeat,

funky Red Hot Chili Peppers down to the smashing hard rock classics, KISS. The six members of the band passionately struggle to make their way in the music world, each with the astounding talent they bring to the stage paired with their own unique sound arising from their individual music backgrounds. Aside from playing covers, SMH always insists on playing a few originals, which are yet to be categorized under a genre, being a mishmash of all the band membersâ€™ music tastes. The best part is, SMH always plays for a social cause. Their music quite literally makes the world a slightly better place, since any money they earn from their performances is donated to charity of their choice.
